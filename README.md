# Festio
A Centralized College Event Management Platform

## Problem Statement

College event information is often shared through fragmented channels such as WhatsApp groups, posters, and social media. This leads to several challenges:

- Event details are scattered across multiple platforms  
- Low student awareness and participation  
- Poor coordination and organizational inefficiencies  
- Data loss and communication gaps  
- Inefficiency during large-scale or inter-college events  

These issues highlight the need for a **centralized and reliable event management solution**.

## Proposed Solution

**Festio** is a centralized digital platform designed to simplify and streamline college event management.

### Key Features:
- Centralized platform for all college events  
- Easy event discovery for students  
- Simplified event creation and management for organizers  
- Centralized event information and registrations  
- Efficient tracking and communication  
- Increased student participation rates  
- Reduced organizational complexity, especially for large and inter-college events  

## Objective

Festio aims to bridge the communication gap between students and event organizers by providing a single, unified platform that improves participation, coordination, and overall event experience.
